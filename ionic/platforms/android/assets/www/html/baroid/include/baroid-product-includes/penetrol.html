PENETROL water miscible, non-ionic wetting agent is designed to help counteract the sticking tendencies of clay.

Applications/Functions

  • Can reduce or eliminate bit balling

  • Can reduce surface tension of drilling fluid to allow faster chip removal without continuously grinding the hard shale formations

  • Can improve drilling efficiency by preferentially coating the bottom-hole assembly and drill string

  • Can minimize differential sticking

  • Can increase bit life and reduce drill pipe and bottom-hole assembly wear
