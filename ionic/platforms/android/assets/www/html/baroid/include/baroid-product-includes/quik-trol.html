QUIK-TROL modified natural cellulosic polymer provides filtration control in most water-based drilling fluids. QUIK-TROL polymer, when added to a QUIK-GEL slurry, yields a drilling mud system suitable for drilling in sandy formation. QUIK-TROL polymer can be added to vegetable or mineral oil to provide an oil-based fluid suspension, which can be poured into drill string directly. QUIK-TROL polymer is also used in air/gel-foam drilling.
